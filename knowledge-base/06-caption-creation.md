# CAPTION CREATION KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Caption Structure for Meta Ads

### 1. Hook (First Line - Scroll Stopper)
- Keep it short (max 1 line)
- Evoke emotion, curiosity, or aspiration
- Examples:
  - "Ever wonder what calm smells like?"
  - "Luxury that you can smell and feel."
  - "Turn any space into your sanctuary."

### 2. Product Value / Benefits (2-3 USPs)
- Show what makes the product worth buying now
- Blend emotional appeal with functional credibility
- Common Zennya USPs:
  - Silent operation
  - Cold air diffusion technology
  - Waterless and heatless
  - Long-lasting scent
  - Pure, undiluted essential oils

### 3. Offer or Seasonal Hook
- Add urgency and connect to current promo
- Examples:
  - "Up to 35% OFF this 11.11 + free Christmas Tree scent"
  - "Perfect for gifting â€” for others or for yourself"

### 4. Call to Action (CTA)
- End decisively, action-oriented
- Examples:
  - "Shop now and elevate your everyday."
  - "Tap to claim your 11.11 deal."
  - "Bring home your calm today."

## Writing Guidelines
- Include 2-3 USPs per caption
- Keep it short: 3-4 lines or 40-60 words
- First 2 lines are crucial (appear before "See More")
- Mirror the creative visual tone
- Balance clarity with aspiration
