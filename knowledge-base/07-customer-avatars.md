# CUSTOMER AVATARS & INSIGHTS

## High-Level Segments

### 1. Wellness-Focused Individuals (Maya)
**Age:** 28-35  
**Profile:** Health-conscious professionals  
**Pain Points:** Stress, poor sleep  
**Motivations:** Natural wellness, self-care

### 2. Home Ambiance Enthusiasts (Sophia)
**Age:** 30-40  
**Profile:** Homemakers or young professionals  
**Pain Points:** Wants luxury home atmosphere  
**Motivations:** Impress guests, create spa-like space

### 3. Busy Urban Professionals (Carlo)
**Age:** 25-45  
**Profile:** Stressed city-dwellers  
**Pain Points:** Long work hours, needs relaxation  
**Motivations:** Quick stress relief, sleep improvement

### 4. Corporate & Commercial Buyers
**Profile:** Spas, hotels, gyms, offices  
**Goal:** Create signature ambiance  
**Purchases:** High-capacity diffusers, bulk oils

## Demographics
- Gender: 70% female, 30% male
- Age: 25-45 years
- Income: Upper-middle to high-income (A/B class)
- Location: Metro Manila (NCR) 78%, Calabarzon 11%, Davao 8%

3. Loved the product design (14%)
4. Wanted to upgrade from cheaper diffuser (12.2%)
