# TARGET MARKET

## Geographic Focus
**Primary:** Metro Manila (NCR) - 78%
- Makati, BGC, Quezon City, Pasig, Mandaluyong

**Secondary:** Calabarzon (11%), Davao (8%), Other (3%)

## Demographics
- **Age:** 25-45 years
- **Gender:** 70% female, 30% male
- **Income:** Upper-middle to high-income (A/B class)
- **Living:** Condos/apartments (35%), Large houses (40%), Business (25%)

## Psychographics
**Values:**
- Wellness and self-care
- Quality and authenticity
- Premium home aesthetics
- Natural and safe products

**Lifestyle:**
- Health-conscious
- Design-aware
- Tech-comfortable
- Busy but values downtime

**Shopping Behavior:**
- Researches before buying
- Reads reviews and testimonials
- Influenced by social proof
- Willing to invest in quality

## Market Segments
- **Primary (60%):** Personal home use
- **Secondary (25%):** Business/commercial
- **Tertiary (15%):** Gifts & special occasions

## Positioning Statement
"Zennya Essentials is the premium aromatherapy brand for wellness-focused Filipinos who demand the highest quality essential oils and innovative diffuser technology to transform their spaces into sanctuaries of calm, focus, and well-being."
