# VIDEO CREATION KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Core Creative Philosophy
- Cinematic, polished, and atmospheric
- Every frame should feel like part of a film or luxury campaign
- Movement, lighting, and sound should evoke emotion

## Structure & Story Arc
1. Hook/Atmosphere (0-10s): Establish mood and intrigue
2. Problem/Desire Setup (10-25s): Connect emotionally
3. Product Reveal (25-45s): Introduce product naturally
4. Benefits & Experience (45-70s): Demonstrate transformation
5. Resolution & CTA (70-90s): Deliver key message + offer

## Visual Direction
- Color Palette: Warm neutrals, whites, blacks, subtle brand tones
- Lighting: Natural sunlight or studio lighting with soft diffusion
- Camera Movement: Smooth dolly, slider, or gimbal shots
- Transitions: Elegant crossfades, match cuts
- Framing: Symmetry, depth, composition

## Technical Specs
- Platform: TikTok/IG Reels - 30-60s, 9:16, 1080x1920
- Platform: YouTube/Website - 60-90s, 16:9, 1920x1080
- Always include captions for accessibility
