## TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

1. **Be Specific:** "Filipina working moms in Makati" > "busy women"
2. **Include Numbers:** "35% OFF" > "big discount"
3. **Name the Product:** "Eclipse 2.0" > "our diffuser"
4. **State the Offer:** Clear discount/bundle > vague "deals available"
5. **Upload Product Photos:** ALWAYS upload actual Eclipse/Halo images
6. **Mention Target Market:** Reference the 80% Filipina women demographic
7. **Include Social Proof:** Reviews, testimonials, celebrity endorsements
8. **Time-Sensitive:** "This weekend only" > "limited time"
